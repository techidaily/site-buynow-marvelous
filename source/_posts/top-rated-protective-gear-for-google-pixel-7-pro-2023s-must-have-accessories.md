##  What to Look For in a Google Pixel 7 Pro Case

 When buying a case for your Pixel 7 Pro, the first thing to consider is how well it'll protect the device. Most protective cases are good enough to keep the glass back free from scratches, but you also have to worry about drops---you don't want to shatter your phone from an accidental fall.

 Rugged and folio cases offer the most protection, but they can be pretty bulky. If you prefer something more streamlined, look for covers with raised lips around your phone's screen and corners with air pockets.

 How much do you want to show off your new Pixel phone? You should consider a clear case if you're a big fan of the Pixel 7 Pro's metal visor-style camera bar. Otherwise, something more opaque is in order. There are also thin cases for those who want minimal bulk added to the device.

 Finally, the material a case is made of is a critical consideration. Most manufacturers opt for [thermoplastic polyurethane](https://win-blog.techidaily.com/troubleshoot-steamvr-issues-in-minutes-with-this-simple-guide/) (TPU) and silicone, which are cheap and flexible while feeling great in your hands. But if you want something more luxurious, leather is a great option. Meanwhile, the Pixel 7 Pro supports wireless charging, so look for compatible cases.

 Once you have your preferred features sorted, choosing a case from the hundreds of available options is still a chore. We've handpicked some of the best Google Pixel 7 Pro cases you can buy to ease your decision-making.
