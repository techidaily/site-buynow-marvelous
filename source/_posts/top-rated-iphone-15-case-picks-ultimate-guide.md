##  What to Look For in an iPhone 15 Plus Case in 2023

 The design differences between the iPhone 14 Plus and iPhone 15 Plus aren’t huge, so you may be tempted to try to reuse your old case. While the old case may somewhat fit, the new model is slightly smaller, so you won’t get a good fit. You also can’t guarantee the buttons will line up, so you’ll want to spring for a new case.

 One of the most important decisions to make when buying a case is the form factor, as this can heavily affect the day-to-day usability of your phone. For example, a slim case doesn’t offer the most protection but makes for an easier fit in your pocket. Folio-style cases offer additional screen protection, as well as letting you prop your phone up like a tablet for watching videos.

 You’ll also want to think about the material your case uses. If you’re just thinking about protection, [thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU)](https://win-blog.techidaily.com/troubleshoot-steamvr-issues-in-minutes-with-this-simple-guide/) offers plenty of resistance against damage from drops, but doesn’t look great. Plastic composite offers more protection, while other materials like leather offer a balance between protection and aesthetics.

 Where and how you use your phone also deserves a fair amount of consideration, as while every case offers protection compared to no case at all, they’re not all built equal. If you’re frequently taking your phone to places most other people wouldn’t dare, you may want to consider a rugged case. These are bulkier but offer more layers of protection to keep your phone extra safe.

 Of course, which case you buy also depends on how much you want to spend. While you can easily spend upwards of $50 on a case, you don’t have to. Affordable cases can still offer plenty of protection; you’ll just want to make sure you’re buying from a well-known and trusted brand.

 Finally, think about the type of look you’re going for. A clear case gives you a look at your phone’s finish while offering protection. On the other hand, some cases let you add an entirely different style to your phone, depending on what you’re looking for.

### Can I use my iPhone 14 Plus case with the iPhone 15 Plus?

 No. While you may be able to fit an iPhone 15 Plus into a case designed for the iPhone 14 Plus, the newer model is smaller overall (albeit by fractions of a millimeter) and shaped slightly differently, so you won’t get a perfect fit.

### Can I use an iPhone 15 Pro Max case with the iPhone 15 Plus?

 No. Despite the name, the overall size of the iPhone 15 Pro Max is just a bit smaller than the iPhone 15 Plus, and it has a larger camera array on the back, so the cases aren’t interchangeable.
